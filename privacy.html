Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Fishcamper respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

The app may store user-created content locally on your device, including saved fishing locations, notes, favorites and photos.

Photos

If you choose to add photos to fishing locations, the photos remain on your device unless you actively share them.

Location Data

Fishcamper may use your device location to improve map functionality and show nearby places. Location data is not stored on our servers.

Purchases

Premium purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Payment information is handled by Apple, Google and RevenueCat.

Data Storage

Fishcamper does not require account creation and does not maintain a user database.

Contact
